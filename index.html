Why Nashville Software School?

My experience at Nashville Software School represents the happy convergence of my past and my future selves. My experiences as a Latin teacher and project manager were fulfilling and rewarding, yet an enduring curiosity about software development led me to join this community of driven, passionate, hard-working individuals striving to produce work of surpassing quality and creativity.

Why I Love Software Development

Humility, a hunger to learn, a willingness to work hard, and flexibility are all characteristics I gleaned from over a decade as a Latin teacher and half a decade as a project manager in residential construction. NSS has tempered and sharpened those positive aspects of my personality and taught me how to bring out the best in my teammates as well.

Another part of software development I appreciate fully is the ability to indulge my desire to be an eternal student. I relish the idea of learning new technology stacks and becoming a true polyglot, in both the ancient and modern sense. Two years ago, I began this journey with a basic Web Development class at MTSU out of pure curiosity, and I found my new vocation. I look forward to the intensive planning sessions, complex problems to solve, teamwork, and victories to come.

Wise Owl Latin

Built with Angular, JavaScript, Bootstrap 3, and Firebase, this educational video application first allows users to sign in via Google Authentication. A new user can then change his or her avatar and username, which are persisted in Firebase. On click of learning topics in the side navigation, the application runs calls to the database to return related videos and learning games. Users can add a video to favorites, remove one from favorites, and the application protects against duplications.
