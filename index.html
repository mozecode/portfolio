Why Nashville Software School?

My experience at Nashville Software School represents the happy convergence of my past and my future selves. My experiences as a Latin teacher and project manager were fulfilling and rewarding, yet curiosity and an enduring love for software development led me to join this community of driven, passionate, hard-working individuals striving to produce work of surpassing quality and creativity.

What I Love About Software Development

One thing I love about working as a developer apprentice is that all the lessons Iâ€™ve learned in other fields carry over so easily to our development teams, and yet I still have a tremendous opportunity to grow each day. Humility, a hunger to learn, a willingness to work hard, and flexibility are all characteristics I gleaned from over a decade as a Latin teacher and half a decade as a project manager in residential construction. NSS has tempered and sharpened those positive aspects of my personality and taught me how to bring out the best in my teammates as well.

Another part of software development I appreciate fully is the ability to indulge my desire to be an eternal student. I relish the idea of learning new technology stacks and becoming a true polyglot, in both the ancient and modern sense. Two years ago, I began this journey with a basic Web Development class at MTSU out of pure curiosity, and I found my vocation. I look forward to the intensive planning, complex problems to solve, teamwork, and victories to come.
